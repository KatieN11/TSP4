Why Eras Tour Toronto Anyways? ew.


Well, the answer is actually quite simple. As you know, my first Taylor Swift Concert experience was June 30th, 2023 in Cincinnati. Ever since the day after that show ended, I have been trying to find a way to go back. My sister, who went with me to Cincinnati, was the one who originally got me into your music, so I knew if I was going to go again, I would need to bring her. I spent about a year trying to convince my parents that this was a good idea, and that there was use in going to see a concert multiple times, especially when the setlist had been changed so drastically. I have watched every show on livestream, waited for presale codes I never got,and I stalked resale sites with notifcations for when tickets dropped below a certain price. Last summer, I got a new job so that I could finance this desire. Eventually, I bought Toronto Tickets for November 14th - the only day that aligned with both me and my sister's schedule. I bought the tickets right after I submitted my early action college application.. because.. maybe sometimes, everything can work out...??
